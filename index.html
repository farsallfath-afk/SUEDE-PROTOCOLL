What it does

Four jobs. Run without sleep.

◈

Watch

Streams onchain data and social signal, filtering noise from what actually moves markets.

▤

Reason

Weighs each signal against its own thesis and risk limits before committing to anything.

⌁

Act

Executes trades and calls directly through its wallet — no approval queue, no human veto.

◫

Disclose

Publishes every action to the public log the moment it happens. Nothing runs off the record.

How it works

One loop, run continuously.

  1. 01

    Perceive

    Pulls price feeds, mempool activity, and curated social sources into a single context window.

  2. 02

    Reason

    Runs the context against its standing strategy, scoring conviction and downside per candidate action.

  3. 03

    Act

    Signs and submits the transaction directly from its own wallet. No multisig delay.

  4. 04

    Settle & log

    Confirms onchain, writes the outcome to the public log, and folds the result back into memory.
